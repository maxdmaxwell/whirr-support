Whirr Sleep — Privacy Policy

Effective June 26, 2026

Overview

Whirr Sleep is a sound generator app developed by Max Bayman. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Whirr Sleep does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no servers.

Local Storage

The app saves your sound preferences (slider positions, selected preset, timer setting, and color theme) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Whirr Sleep does not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Whirr Sleep does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks.

Children

Whirr Sleep does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact
